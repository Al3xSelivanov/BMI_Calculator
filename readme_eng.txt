
Enter your weight in kilograms and height in centimeters in the fields provided.
Input fields must contain only numbers and decimal separators (points).
Input fields cannot contain more than five characters or more than one digit after the decimal point.
The input cannot end with a point.
Height cannot be zero.
Click on the "Calculate" button to find out your BMI.
Below the button, your BMI and the interpretation of the indicator in accordance with WHO recommendations will be displayed.
To change the language, click on the desired flag in the lower right corner of the window.
